# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers research user needs, create user flows, design interfaces, and validate usability. They collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to ensure features are intuitive and meet user expectations.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define user flows and information architecture
- Collaborate on accessibility and responsive design
- Participate in design reviews and handoff to developers
- Validate implementation matches design intent

### Goals
- Deliver delightful, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support time
- Ensure design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- Weekly sync with Product and Engineering
- Design specs, style guides, and component documentation
- Usability testing reports and insights

---

## Tech Lead / Architect

### Role Summary
Tech Leads and Architects define technical direction, ensure architectural soundness, and guide the team on complex design decisions. They balance technical excellence with practical delivery constraints.

### Responsibilities
- Define and communicate technical architecture and standards
- Lead technical design reviews and spike investigations
- Mentor developers on best practices and patterns
- Make key technology decisions and trade-offs
- Identify and mitigate technical debt and scalability risks
- Ensure code quality and maintainability across the codebase

### Goals
- Build scalable, maintainable, and secure systems
- Enable developer productivity through sound architecture
- Balance innovation with stability and delivery speed

### Typical Communication
- Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)
- Technical design documents and diagrams
- Code review guidance and mentorship
- Weekly tech sync and planning sessions

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure, and monitoring systems. They enable reliable and efficient deployment processes and ensure operational excellence.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure as code and cloud resources
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting
- Respond to infrastructure incidents and perform root cause analysis
- Optimize deployment processes and automation
- Ensure security and compliance in infrastructure

### Goals
- Minimize deployment time and manual intervention
- Maximize system reliability and uptime
- Enable rapid feedback loops for developers

### Typical Communication
- Incident reports and post-mortems
- Infrastructure change notifications
- Performance and capacity planning updates
- Runbooks and operational documentation

---

## Security Champion

### Role Summary
Security Champions advocate for security best practices within the team, conduct security reviews, and ensure vulnerabilities are identified and addressed proactively.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security reviews of design and code
- Identify and track security vulnerabilities
- Advocate for secure coding practices and standards
- Coordinate with central security team on assessments
- Ensure compliance with security policies and frameworks
- Lead threat modeling and risk assessment activities

### Goals
- Prevent security incidents before they occur
- Build security awareness across the team
- Ensure compliance with security standards and regulations

### Typical Communication
- Security review findings and remediation plans
- Threat models and risk assessments
- Security training sessions and knowledge sharing
- Vulnerability reports and tracking updates

---

## Business Stakeholder

### Role Summary
Business Stakeholders represent the business interests, provide strategic direction, and make go/no-go decisions for projects. They ensure initiatives align with business goals and provide necessary resources.

### Responsibilities
- Define business objectives and success criteria
- Provide strategic direction and funding approval
- Make critical prioritization and trade-off decisions
- Review progress and approve major milestones
- Ensure alignment with company strategy and compliance
- Champion the project across the organization

### Goals
- Maximize business value and ROI
- Ensure projects deliver on strategic objectives
- Maintain visibility and control over investments

### Typical Communication
- Monthly steering committee meetings
- Milestone reviews and go/no-go decisions
- Executive status reports and dashboards
- Business case reviews and funding approvals

---

## QA Lead / Test Engineer

### Role Summary
QA Leads and Test Engineers ensure quality through test planning, test automation, and validation of acceptance criteria. They work closely with developers and product managers to prevent defects and validate releases.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain test plans and test cases
- Design and implement automated test suites
- Perform manual testing for complex scenarios and edge cases
- Validate acceptance criteria and Definition of Done
- Track and triage defects and quality metrics
- Coordinate release testing and sign-off

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Maintain high test coverage and quality metrics
- Enable fast, confident releases through automation

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and test case documentation
- Bug reports and quality dashboards
- Release readiness assessments and sign-offs
- Weekly quality sync with PM and Engineering
